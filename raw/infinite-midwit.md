# Infinite Midwit

## Key Thesis

LLMs = pure **objective intelligence**. They can solve bounded problems with verifiable solutions, but cannot solve **subjective problems** — vague, squishy problems without clear right answers.

## The Wall

There's a hole in AI capabilities "as big as it was in 2022" — subjective intelligence. This wall cannot be overcome by scaling (more data, more compute).

**Evidence:**
- AI can write grammatically correct essays but they "vaguely suck"
- "I drag my eyes across the words and I feel nothing"
- No amount of objective intelligence trades for subjective intelligence (not Catan rules)

## Madame Stats & Mr. Encyclopedia

LLM = unlimited access to friendly Madame Stats (stats) + Mr. Encyclopedia (knowledge).

But they can't tell you:
1. If your idea is **boring** (most important feedback)
2. If your project matters
3. Any subjective judgment

## Bottleneck Blindness

When you remove one bottleneck (e.g., cheap research), you hit another (motivation, ideas, direction). **You can't trade objective intelligence for other bottlenecks.**

## Testing Implications

1. **Test AI the same way** — distinguish objective vs subjective requirements
2. **AI writing tests** — grammar/syntax checkable, but "alive writing" not
3. **Bottleneck thinking** — find what's actually blocking quality, not just adding more testing
4. **Human judgment still required** for subjective quality
