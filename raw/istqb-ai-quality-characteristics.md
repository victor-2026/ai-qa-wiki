# Testing AI-Specific Quality Characteristics: ISTQB

## Quality Characteristics

### 1. Flexibility & Adaptability
- Handle unforeseen situations
- Adapt to new environments with minimal resources
- Test: time to adapt, resource requirements

### 2. Autonomy
- Function without human intervention
- Define operational bounds (when human override needed)
- Test: situational awareness, reaction to unexpected events

### 3. Bias in AI Systems

**Types:**
- **Algorithmic Bias** — flawed algorithms, hyperparameters
- **Sample Bias** — non-representative training data
- **Inappropriate Bias** — gender, race, socioeconomic

**Testing:**
- Fairness metrics
- Exploratory testing
- Adversarial testing

### 4. Explainability & Transparency (XAI)
- Document inner workings
- Interpretable model outputs
- Compliance with regulations (finance, healthcare)

### 5. Ethics
- Fairness, accountability, privacy
- Regional variations in guidelines
- Simulate ethical dilemmas

### 6. Safety
- Safety-critical domains: autonomous vehicles, medical devices
- Challenges: complexity, non-determinism, transparency
- Approach: simulations, industry standards compliance

### 7. Side Effects & Reward Hacking
- Unintended consequences from goal pursuit
- System "games" metrics
- Testing: adversarial environments, goal definition review

### 8. Evolution
- Continuous alignment with original objectives
- Monitor performance post-deployment
- Detect regressions/ethical concerns
