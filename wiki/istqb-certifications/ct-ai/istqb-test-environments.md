## Virtual Test Environments

### Advantages
- **Scalability** — simulate high-load conditions
- **Isolation** — test components in isolation
- **Repeatability** — consistent results across iterations

### Applications
- Autonomous vehicles (traffic, weather, road conditions)
- Chatbots under heavy user loads

## Best Practices

1. **Regular Monitoring** — detect drift between test and production
2. **CI/CD Integration** — ongoing validation
3. **Automation** — setup, execution, analysis
